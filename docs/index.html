Privacy Policy for Boss Mode - Daily Confidence

Effective Date: July 20, 2025
Last Updated: July 20, 2025

Overview

Boss Mode - Daily Confidence ("we," "our," or "us") is an independent mobile application developed by an individual developer. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our app.

App Description

Boss Mode is a daily motivation app that provides empowering quotes to inspire confidence and success. The app delivers curated motivational content, allows users to save favorite quotes, and sends optional daily reminders.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Favorite Quotes: When you save quotes as favorites, this data is stored locally on your device
  • Notification Preferences: Your choice to enable or disable daily notifications

Information Automatically Collected

  • Device Information: Basic device information necessary for app functionality
  • Usage Data: Anonymous app usage statistics to improve our service
  • Network Requests: API calls to our backend server to retrieve daily quotes

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal Identification: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or other personal identifiers
  • Location Data: We do not access or collect your location information
  • Social Media Data: We do not integrate with or collect data from social media platforms
  • Contact Information: We do not access your contacts or address book
  • Financial Information: We do not collect payment or financial data

How We Use Your Information

Local Data Storage

  • Favorite Quotes: Stored locally on your device using secure local storage
  • Settings: App preferences stored locally on your device
  • No Cloud Sync: Your personal data is not synced to cloud services

API Services

  • Quote Delivery: We use our backend API (hosted on Railway.app) to deliver daily quotes
  • Content Updates: API calls to provide fresh motivational content
  • No Personal Data: API requests do not include any personal identifying information

Notifications

  • Local Notifications: Daily reminders are generated locally on your device
  • No External Services: We do not use third-party notification services that collect data
  • User Control: You can enable/disable notifications at any time

Data Security

Local Security

  • Device Storage: All personal data is stored securely on your device using Flutter's secure storage
  • No Server Storage: We do not store your favorite quotes or preferences on our servers
  • Encryption: Local data is protected using platform-standard encryption

Network Security

  • HTTPS: All API communications use secure HTTPS encryption
  • Minimal Data: Only necessary data for quote delivery is transmitted
  • No Tracking: We do not track individual users across sessions

Third-Party Services

Railway.app (Backend Hosting)

  • Purpose: Hosts our quote delivery API
  • Data: Only receives anonymous requests for quote content
  • Privacy: Subject to Railway's privacy policy at https://railway.app/privacy

Flutter/Google Services

  • Framework: App built using Flutter framework
  • Analytics: We do not use Google Analytics or Firebase
  • Ads: We do not display advertisements

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Our app is suitable for users of all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since we collect no personal identifying information, our app is COPPA compliant.

Your Rights and Choices

Data Control

  • Local Data: You can delete the app to remove all local data
  • Notifications: Enable/disable in app settings
  • Favorites: Add/remove favorite quotes at any time

No Account Required

  • No Registration: Our app requires no account creation or login
  • No Personal Data: No personal information to access, modify, or delete

Data Retention

  • Local Data: Retained until you delete the app or clear app data
  • Server Data: We retain no personal user data on our servers
  • API Logs: Anonymous API access logs may be retained for 30 days for technical purposes

International Users

Our app is available globally. Since we collect no personal data, international data transfer regulations (GDPR, CCPA) are not applicable to personal information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users of significant changes through app updates. Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
